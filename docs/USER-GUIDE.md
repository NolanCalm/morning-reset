## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Go to [morningreset.app](https://morningreset.app) (or your local instance)
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email (check your inbox)
5. You're ready to start!

### Sign In

If you already have an account:

1. Click "Sign In" in the top-right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Your First Check-In

After signing in, you'll be taken to the dashboard.

### How to Check In

1. **Look at today's date** - Displayed in the top-right corner
2. **Enter your wake time** - Use the time picker to select when you woke up
3. **Click "Complete Today's Reset"** - That's it!

### What Happens After Check-In

- Your streak count increases by 1
- Your check-in is saved to your history
- You'll see a success message with your current streak
- You can check in again tomorrow

### Can I Check In Multiple Times?

No. Each day allows only one check-in. If you made a mistake, contact support (or update your wake time in settings—coming soon).

## Understanding Your Streak

Morning Reset uses a **forgiving streak system**. Here's what that means:

### Traditional Streaks (Punitive)
Most habit trackers reset your streak to 0 if you miss a day:
- ✅ Day 1: 1 day streak
- ✅ Day 2: 2 day streak
- ❌ Day 3: Miss a day → Streak resets to **0**
- This feels discouraging and kills motivation

### Morning Reset Streaks (Forgiving)
Your streak doesn't reset to 0 if you miss a day:
- ✅ Day 1: 1 day streak
- ✅ Day 2: 2 day streak
- ❌ Day 3: Miss a day → Streak stays at **2**
- ✅ Day 4: Check in → Streak becomes **3**

### When Does Your Streak Reset?

Your streak resets only if:
- You haven't checked in for 7+ days
- You explicitly restart your streak in settings (coming soon)

This means:
- **Missing 1-2 days?** No problem, streak continues
- **Missing 3-6 days?** Still okay, streak continues
- **Missing 7+ days?** Streak resets to 0

### Streak Milestones

Celebrate your progress with milestone badges:

- **7 Days:** 🗡️ Week Warrior
- **14 Days:** 🏆 Month Master
- **30 Days:** 🏅 Streak Champion
- **60 Days:** 👑 Habit Hero
- **100 Days:** 🌟 Morning Legend

## Viewing Your Progress

The Progress Dashboard shows your habit-building journey over time.

### Weekly View (Default)

- Shows the last 7 days
- Each day displays:
  - Day name (Mon, Tue, Wed, etc.)
  - Check-in status (green = completed, gray = missed)
  - Streak count on that day
- Achievement badges appear on days with milestones

### Monthly View

- Shows the entire month
- Same data as weekly view, but for 30 days
- Great for spotting patterns and trends

### Key Metrics

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see:

- **Current Streak:** How many days you've checked in this streak
- **Longest Streak:** Your personal record
- **Completion Rate:** Percentage of days you've checked in (all time)

### What's a "Good" Completion Rate?

- **70%+**: Excellent! You're building a strong habit
- **50-70%**: Good! Some ups and downs, but consistent overall
- **30-50%**: Okay! Room for improvement
- **<30%**: Needs focus. Try setting smaller goals.

## Setting Your Preferences

Customize Morning Reset to fit your lifestyle.

### Wake Goal Time

Set the time you aim to wake up each day:
- This is your target, not a deadline
- Helps you build consistency
- Coming soon: reminders to wake up at this time

### Reset Duration

How long your morning reset routine takes:
- **15 minutes**: Quick reset (stretch, water)
- **30 minutes**: Standard reset (stretch, water, light)
- **45 minutes**: Extended reset (add exercise or meditation)

### Timezone

Make sure your timezone is correct:
- Ensures check-ins are recorded for the right day
- Prevents confusion when traveling
- Default: Auto-detected from your browser

## Tips for Success

### 1. Start Small

If you're not a morning person:
- **Week 1**: Just check in (no pressure to wake up early)
- **Week 2**: Set a realistic wake goal (e.g., 30 minutes earlier than usual)
- **Week 3**: Add a simple reset activity (stretch, water)
- **Week 4**: Build your full routine

### 2. Create a Morning Environment

Set up your space for success:
- **Place your phone away from bed** (so you have to get up to check in)
- **Prepare water the night before** (first thing in the morning)
- **Open curtains or use a lamp** (light exposure signals your body)
- **Have a playlist ready** (uplifting music to start the day)

### 3. Connect to Your "Why"

Ask yourself: Why do I want to build this habit?
- More energy?
- Less brain rot?
- Better work-life balance?
- More time with family?

Write it down and look at it every morning.

### 4. Be Forgiving

Remember: Morning Reset is forgiving!
- Miss a day? No problem.
- Miss a week? Still okay.
- Just get back to it when you can.

Anxiety about perfection kills motivation. Progress over perfection.

### 5. Celebrate Wins

Every check-in is a win:
- 🎉 Check in today → 1 win
- 🗡️ 7-day streak → Week Warrior!
- 🏆 14-day streak → Month Master!
- 🌟 100-day streak → Morning Legend!

Share your progress with friends or in the community!

## FAQ

### Q: What if I forget to check in?

**A:** It happens! Your streak continues for up to 7 days. Just check in when you remember.

### Q: Can I check in for yesterday if I forgot?

**A:** Not currently. We're working on retroactive check-ins for v1.2.

### Q: What if I'm traveling and in a different timezone?

**A:** Set your timezone in settings. Check-ins are recorded based on your set timezone, not your location.

### Q: Can I have multiple streaks?

**A:** Not yet. We're considering "challenges" or "goals" for v2.0 where you can track multiple habits.

### Q: Is my data private?

**A:** Yes! We use Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS). Your data is encrypted and only you can access it. See our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md) for details.

### Q: Can I export my data?

**A:** Yes! We'll add data export in v1.1 (CSV or JSON format).

### Q: What if I want to delete my account?

**A:** You can delete your account in settings (coming in v1.1). This permanently removes all your data.

### Q: Is this free?

**A:** Yes! Morning Reset is free to use. We'll consider premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always be free.
